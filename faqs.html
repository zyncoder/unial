Speak to Lead is a semester-long, in-person course that builds students’ communication confidence, soft skills, and presentation ability. Modeled on Behavioral and Communication Interventions (BCI) used in top corporates, it prepares students for real-world professional environments.

This program is designed for colleges and universities. Entire batches or departments can enroll their students, ideally in the final or pre-final years, to make them interview- and workplace-ready.

  • Public speaking & storytelling
  • Email & verbal communication etiquette
  • Interview & group discussion mastery
  • Voice modulation & body language
  • Confidence under pressure

The course is conducted in person over a full semester, with weekly sessions of 1.5 hours. Trainers from Unial visit the campus to facilitate interactive, practice-driven modules.
