Program Overview

Structured Communication Training for Placement-Ready Students

Speak to Lead is a semester-long, in-person program designed to build students' communication, confidence, and professional presence. Modeled on Behavioral and Communication Interventions (BCI) used in leading corporations, the course equips students with practical skills that directly improve placement outcomes.

Our program fits seamlessly into the academic timetable and is designed and delivered by trainers with experience in corporate-grade interventions. The curriculum is aligned with placement and career development goals.

Focused on measurable transformation, not just theory - we prepare students to excel in interviews, group discussions, presentations, and professional interactions that matter for career success.

Program Structure

Comprehensive 4.5-Month Journey

  • Semester: 5th (starting August)
  • Duration: 4.5 months
  • Frequency: 2 periods per week
  • Mode: In-person training
  • Assignments: Practical exercises
  • Mock Sessions: 2 rounds of GDs and interviews

Students will create and present pitch decks, showcase academic or startup projects, and handle Q&A sessions with confidence. The program includes assignments, two rounds of mock GDs and interviews, pitch presentations, and personalized trainer feedback.

Our structured approach ensures students develop both technical communication skills and the confidence to apply them in real-world placement scenarios.
